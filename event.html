Recent Programs and Outreach Activities

Girl Child Health Education Outreach

A health-education outreach held in public secondary schools in Akwa Ibom State where sanitary kits were distributed to more than 1,000 female students. (Sponsored by: Oriental Energy Resources Limited)

June 2022

Akwa Ibom State, Nigeria

Community Health Outreach (Mbo LGA)

Five-day medical outreach in Mbo LGA, Akwa Ibom State: antenatal kits and treated mosquito nets distributed; the program provided free medical services to over 5,000 people. (Sponsored by: Oriental Energy Resources Limited)

October 2022

Mbo LGA, Akwa Ibom, Nigeria

Community Food Share — 1st Edition

First edition of the Community Food Share in Gwarinpa Estate, Abuja. About 200 children received food packs and water during the festive outreach.

December 2023

Gwarinpa Estate, Abuja, Nigeria

Visit to Mother Theresa Children Home

Donation visit to Mother Theresa Children Home in Abuja — the Foundation donated raw food items and a cash gift to support the children's needs.

June 2024

Abuja, Nigeria

Girl Child Health Education Outreach — 2nd Edition

Second edition of the Girl Child Health Education Outreach; the Founder/CEO participated as Resource Person. Over 1,000 girls benefited from sanitary kits and health education across schools in Akwa Ibom State. (Sponsored by: Oriental Energy Resources Limited)

October 2024

Akwa Ibom State, Nigeria

Community Food Share — 2nd Edition

Second edition held in Anguwar Sarki Madallah, Suleja (Niger State). Approximately 500 children were fed with cooked food and drinking water as part of the Foundation's food-share program.

December 2024

Suleja, Niger State, Nigeria
