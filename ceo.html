Founder & CEO

Lady Imoh Ekpo

Lady Imoh Ekpo is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Famohs Place Foundation for the Vulnerable. She is a passionate humanitarian with a heart for service, especially to the vulnerable members of society — women, children, widows, and the less privileged. Her leadership reflects compassion, dedication, and a lifelong commitment to improving lives through empowerment, education, healthcare, and social welfare programs.

Lady Imoh Ekpo holds a Bachelor’s degree in Banking and Finance and a Master’s degree in Legislative Studies. Her experience in community engagement, policy understanding, and human development has fueled her drive to create a platform that bridges the gap between opportunity and access for the most vulnerable. She believes that empowerment — not charity — is the key to breaking the cycle of poverty.

Under her leadership, the Foundation has implemented several impactful programs, including girl-child health and education outreaches, community medical missions, food share initiatives, and advocacy campaigns across multiple states in Nigeria. Her vision is to build a society where everyone has the right tools and opportunities to live with dignity and independence.

Known for her humility, transparency, and result-driven leadership, Lady Imoh Ekpo continues to inspire a generation of young Nigerians to become change agents in their communities. She is driven by the belief that true leadership is not about titles but about service to humanity.

Beyond her professional achievements, Lady Imoh Ekpo is a mentor, motivator, and voice of hope for many. She works closely with schools, community leaders, and women’s groups to promote education, health awareness, and economic self-reliance. Her focus on capacity building and collaboration reflects her deep understanding that sustainable development begins at the grassroots.

Through Famohs Place Foundation, she has shown that impactful change is possible when compassion meets consistency. Lady Imoh continues to expand the Foundation’s reach, creating a network of partners and volunteers who share her mission of empowering the vulnerable and restoring dignity to every life touched.

“Service to humanity is the highest calling. Every life we touch is a seed of hope for a better tomorrow.” — Lady Imoh Ekpo
